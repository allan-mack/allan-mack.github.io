Geography:

The highest elevation is 345 feet above sea level in Walton County. Coastal lowlands make up nearly 75% of Florida's entire land.(Britannica, 2022)

Climate:

The southern half of the state is considered tropical, while the northern half is considered subtropical.

Plant Life:

There are thousands of different plants that are documented in Florida, including pine trees, oak trees, palms, and grasses. Almost half of the trees found in United States are found in Florida.

Animal Life:

Some of Florida's most famous animals include the alligator, manatee, Florida panther, and dolphin. There are thousands of bird species that migrate in and out of Florida each year.
